Our Products

Engineering Excellence

Every machine is precision-built to deliver reliable performance under continuous industrial operation.

Decatising Machine

The Decatising Machine delivers unique finish effects with controlled steaming, vacuuming, and wrapper cloth pressure.

Hyrdaulic Jigger

The Hydraulic Maxi Jigger offers automatic temperature control, PLC touch screen operation, and low tension fabric processing for different fabric types.

Gas Singeing Machine

The Gas Singeing Machine features controlled combustion with Double-Jet burners for high intensity blue flame and lower gas consumption.

Calander Machine

The Calander Machine features a strong vibration-free frame and a 5 bowl calandering unit with cotton and hard chromed rollers for smooth fabric finishing.

Automatic Dyeing Jigger

The Automatic Dyeing Jigger Machine offers smooth and efficient performance with synchronized motor drives, PLC controlled functions, and touch screen operation.

Baby Felt Calander

The Baby Felt Calender Machine is designed for superior fabric finishing of cotton, blended, and wool fabrics with smooth, soft, and enhanced surface finish.

HTHP Jigger

The HTHP Jigger is designed for dyeing open width nylon, spandex, silk, cotton, polyester, flax, and blended fabrics.
