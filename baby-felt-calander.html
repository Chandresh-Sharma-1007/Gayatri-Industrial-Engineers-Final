Premium Textile Finishing Equipment

Baby Felt
Calander
Machine

Gayatri Industrial Engineers' Baby Felt Calander Machine delivers superior glazing, smoothing and surface finishing for pure cotton, blended fabrics and wool — combining a precision 19″ mirror-finish main cylinder, adjustable pressure rolls and inverter drive for consistent, vibration-free production efficiency.

Get Quotation
Key Features

Built for Excellence

01
19″ Mirror-Finish Main Cylinder
The main cylinder fabricated at 19″ diameter is properly ground and mirror-finished, running on heavy-duty ball bearings with steam inlet and outlet — delivering flawless, uniform glazing and surface finishing across the full fabric width.
02
Adjustable Pressure Roll System
Two 190 mm dia. pressure rolls with steam flats and a worm & worm gear adjustable pressing system allow precise, controlled nip pressure — accommodating the full range of fabric weights from light cotton to heavy wool blends.
03
Creaseless Fabric Feeding
A swivel tension adjuster, tension binders and a dedicated ebonite scroll roll feed the cloth in a perfectly creaseless manner — ensuring a pristine, distortion-free surface before the fabric contacts the main cylinder and felt.
04
Inverter Drive & Box Plaiter
A 3 H.P. A.C. motor with reduction gearbox and inverter drive powers the main cylinder, while a synchronized heavy-duty box-type plaiter with draw roll delivers the finished fabric smoothly at production speeds up to 1000 m/hr.
Technical Specifications

Salient Features

  • Working Width: 60″ to 84″ — accommodating a wide range of fabric widths for both standard and wide-format textile finishing requirements.
  • Main Body: Fabricated from M.S. plate, M.S. channel and strengthening bars — rigidly constructed for vibration-free running under continuous production conditions.
  • Main Cylinder: Fabricated at 19″ dia., properly ground and mirror-finished, running on heavy-duty ball bearings. Fitted with steam inlet and outlet device for precise heating throughout the cylinder surface.
  • Pressing Rolls: Two nos. pressure rolls of 190 mm dia. with steam flats. Adjustable pressing system operated by worm & worm gear for fine, repeatable nip-pressure control across all fabric weights.
  • Steam Tank: S.S. steam tank with perforated steam pipe fitted at the end to guide the fabric into the machine. Two ebonite rolls provided for smooth fabric entry.
  • Fabric Feeding: Equipped with swivel tension adjuster, tension binders and a separate ebonite scroll roll — feeding cloth in a perfectly creaseless manner to protect delicate fabric surfaces before calandering.
  • Felt Adjustment Roll: One felt adjustment roll of 125 mm dia. on ball bearings — providing accurate felt tracking and tension adjustment for consistent felt contact and even surface finishing results.
  • Drive: Main cylinder driven through a reduction gearbox coupled with a 3 H.P. A.C. motor with inverter drive. Plaiter is driven by chain and chain wheel — all synchronized for smooth, constant-speed operation.
  • Plaiter: Synchronized heavy-duty box-type plaiter provided with necessary draw roll — delivering the finished fabric in neat, consistent folds at production speeds up to 1000 m/hr.
  • Electric Panel: A complete electric control panel is provided — housing all controls for motor, inverter drive, steam temperature and machine speed for safe, easy and centralized operation.
