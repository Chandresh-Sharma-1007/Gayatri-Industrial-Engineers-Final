Advanced Gas Singeing Solutions

Gas Singeing
Machine

Gayatri's Gas Singeing Machine combines a Ceramic Double-Jet burner with a dust-free fabric entry system and automatic flame-width adjustment—delivering a high-intensity blue flame for flawless singeing of natural, regenerated and synthetic fabrics.

Built for Excellence

Controlled Combustion
The constantly evolved Ceremic Double-Jet slot burner ensures a homogenous, high-intensity blue flame for precision singeing of all fabrics types.

Salient Features

Technical Specifications

  • Ceremic Double-Jet Slot Burner : Double-jet burner mounted with ceramic bricks and a heavy-duty aluminum alloy body construction. Designed for durability and superior performance, the burner produces a uniform, high-intensity blue flame, ensuring precise and consistent singeing across all types of fabrics.
  • Brushing / Beating Unit: 2 or 4 roll dust-free fabric entry system with closed extraction chamber and fully sealed entrance section; brushes or beaters on both sides with adjustable fabric contact from zero to maximum.
  • Proportional Mixing Valves: Guarantees a constant gas:air ratio independent of feed volume, ensuring flame quality consistency across all production speeds.
  • Singeing Positions: Different singeing positions can be achieved according to fabric quality, including Tangential Singeing, Singeing on Water-Cooled Roller, and Singeing on Free Guided Fabric.
  • Impregnation Compartment: Available in 10, 15 and 21 meter fabric capacity. Upper and lower rollers fitted externally with ball bearings; high speed up to 120 mtr/min with two-roller squeeze mangles at exit.
  • Fabric Delivery Options: Batchers and plaiters synchronised with the singeing process speed for smooth and uniform fabric handling.
  • Safety Compliance: Conforms to the strictest international safety regulations.
