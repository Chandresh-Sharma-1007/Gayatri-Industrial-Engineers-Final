Salient Features

Technical Specifications

  • Pneumatic Door System: All GAYATRI Jigger doors are fitted with large glass view ports and a pneumatic lowering/lifting arrangement.
  • On-Line Tension Sensing: Continuous tension sensing enables operation from 5 Kg to 50 Kg (30 Kg for smaller models).
  • Spray Washing System: Efficient spray washing above water level achieves thorough fabric cleaning; continuous washing with a washing-by-litre facility maintains accurate total water consumption irrespective of machine speed.
  • Precision Dosing: Dosing at will with linear, progressive and regressive dosing profiles as per process demand; a water flow meter ensures accurate control over water supply at all times.
  • Auto Rocking Device: An automatic rocking device avoids unbalanced batches when the machine is stopped. An auxiliary motor provides constant fabric tension during feeding and easy unloading on large batches.
  • Advanced Diagnostics: Built-in diagnostics alert the operator to malfunctions in real time. Alarm history for the last 50 stoppages facilitates thorough breakdown analysis and preventive maintenance.
  • Dye Compatibility: GAYATRI Jiggers are ideally suited for dyeing with Vat, Reactive, Sulphur and other dye-stuffs; available in models 1000, 500, 300 and 75 with selvedge shifting device as standard on the fully automatic versions.

Outstanding Advantages

Why Choose
Our Machine?

Gayatri's Hydraulic Maxi Jigger combines decades of dyeing process expertise with modern automation — from cushioned start/stop to intelligent diagnostics — to deliver flawless, repeatable results across every batch.

09
Engineering Advantages
  • 01
    Cushioned Start / Slow StopGentle fabric acceleration and deceleration prevents tension shocks, protecting delicate and high-value textiles throughout the full dyeing cycle.
  • 02
    Finger-Tip Tension AdjustmentPrecise fingertip tension control with on-line sensing from 5 Kg to 50 Kg—load-cell closed-loop feedback available for silk and highly tension-sensitive fabrics.
  • 03
    Indirect Heating — No Liquor DilutionIndirect through-heating eliminates direct steam contact with the bath, preventing liquor dilution and ensuring consistent dye concentration throughout the cycle.
  • 04
    Auto Pendulation & Batch ReversingAn automatic rocking device avoids unbalanced batches during standstill; auto batch-reversing and selvedge-shifting are standard on fully automatic models 75 to 1000.
  • 05
    Linear / Progressive / Regressive DosingIntelligent dosing-at-will with three programmable profiles ensures the correct dye or chemical addition rate at every stage of the process.
  • 06
    Water-Saving Continuous WashingWashing-by-litre facility maintains an accurate total water budget irrespective of machine speed, significantly reducing water and effluent treatment costs.
  • 07
    VFD System — 25–50% Energy SavingVariable Frequency Drive drive connected to the power pack cuts installed electrical energy consumption by up to 50%, lowering operational costs on every run.
  • 08
    Advanced Built-In DiagnosticsReal-time malfunction alerts and alarm history for the last 50 stoppages enable fast breakdown analysis and proactive preventive maintenance scheduling.
  • 09
    Safety Before Door OpeningA dedicated safety device fully exhausts steam pressure before any door can be opened, protecting operators in full compliance with international safety standards.
